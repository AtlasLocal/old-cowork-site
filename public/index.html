What is CoWork?

Cowork is 100% independent collaboration. We are an eclectic group of designers, strategists, developers, writers and entrepreneurs. We are not fly-by-night freelancers. We are independent business owners, connected with each other through the space we share and the projects we inevitably develop together. We are cowork.

Where is CoWork?

The city you live in tells a story and we like the story Greenville, SC tells. Certainly, every member of CoWork,
at some point, has lived in or toyed with the idea of the “big city.” We thrive on the small-town aesthetic of Greenville’s historic downtown, the cultivated local art scene and quality people just like ourselves. Come take a look for yourself.
